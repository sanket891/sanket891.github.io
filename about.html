About Me!

Hi All. I'm Sanket. I currently live in the greater Philadelphia area. I currently work as a Data Science research analyst partnering with various functional areas such as finance, digital marketing, HR, marketing and facilities on big data projects.

I’m a technology enthusiast and a data geek. My background is in Economics/ Finance, but I hopped on the data science train in 2012.I think that was one of my best decisions I’ve made in life. Throughout the years I have worked with different industries and produced quantifiable results utilizing decision tree models, regression models, clustering algorithms, text mining, A/B testing and tableau visualizations.

My work spans from solving isolated business questions to building predictive models utilizing the CRISP-DM methodology. The journey starts by working closely with the key stakeholders and identifying the problem to all the way in the end by providing recommendations on a solution. Additionally, I co-led the efforts in the development of the training materials for Microstrategy and Tableau at Wawa.

Some of my competencies include- ability to work independently and in cross functional teams, lead and mentor colleagues, communicate with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

In my free time- I’m currently working with Raspberry Pi’s camera module for face, person and object detection to count foot traffic and to identify objects.
